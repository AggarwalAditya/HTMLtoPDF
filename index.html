1. Objective 

Encourage and assist all our Team Members to acquire any certifications, which will add value to professional career by providing the platform for recognition and enhanced career development. 

 

2. Eligibility 

a. All confirmed team members are eligible. 
b. The certification should be relevant to the current area of work of the team member. 
c. The certification should be pre-approved by the Reporting Manager. 
d. Team members serving notice period are not covered under this policy. 

 

3. Reimbursement

a. On successful completion of the certification, Organisation would reimburse the actual cost of certification or a maximum amount (whichever is less) as mentioned in the next point given below. 

b. A sum of Rs 10,000/- is the maximum that a team member can get reimbursed against successful certifications in the course of 2 years. This amount can be reimbursed for a single or multiple certifications subject to a maximum of Rs 10,000 for 2 years. 

c. Team members are required to submit the supporting documents and the approved Certification Request to the Reporting Manager while claiming the reimbursement. 

d. Any leaves required for preparation and/or appearing for the examination should be planned and informed to the Reporting Manager/Project Manager/Delivery Manager well in advance. 

e. The certification cost will not cover the cost incurred towards the training, reading material, etc. unless training is a mandatory pre-requisite for the certification. 

f. No other associated costs will be reimbursed. 

 

4. Recovery 

Accunity India shall recover 100% of the expense that the organisation incurs per person in the following scenarios:

a. The participant resigns before/within 1 year of certificate completion. 

b. The participant fails to clear the final exam/certification. 

c. The participant does not appear for the exam.
